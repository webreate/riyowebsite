IMPORTANCE OF HYGIENE SERVICES ON NHAI.


A nation's healthy and growing economy is dependent on The National highways of India, but what about its level of hygiene? National Highway which has emerged as a dominant segment of the economy is critical to India's growth story.


It accounts for more than 3.6 percent of India's GDP, or roughly two-thirds of all transport-related contributions to the country's GDP. With a total length of 5.5 million kilometers, India has one of the world's largest road networks.


But the most critical factor that if put into account cannot be ignored is HYGIENE. People in large amount travel across the National Highways therefore they are bound to use the services provided on the National Highways. But the question is are those services really neat and hygienic to prefer?

Although, the Government of India has launched several projects to develop the National highways and road networks but what about the hygiene level of the road and on the roadside on the National Highways?


National Highways, the lifeline of the country, is yet lacking neat, sanitized, and clean washrooms and restrooms facilities. That forces people or travelers to get hygienic food and refreshment across the roadside on the National Highways. This problem is a major issue in our country.



Importance Of Hygiene And Sanitation On National Highways.



Hygiene and sanitation are important on National Highways for a variety of reasons, including personal, social, psychological, and health concerns. Proper hygiene and sanitation help to prevent diseases and infections. Diseases will be greatly reduced if every Highway in our country practices good hygiene measures to maintain a healthy ecosystem and better transportation.


Another critical factor is sanitation. Many of the common diseases mentioned earlier, such as roundworms, spread through the air in the surroundings and infect people. As we are aware that the roadside amenities are usually crowded which increases the risk of infections. We can completely eliminate such diseases, by ensuring proper sanitization in public places so that people do not get infected in public. By identifying diseases and infectious symptoms accordingly can be prevented through proper sanitization.



Need Of Development Of Hygienic Roadside Amenities For Truckers



Over the past years, there has been a significant increase in heavy vehicles such as Trucks, and trailers, on the Road networks. Accidents have increased due to the lack of efficient services on the National Highways. As a result, there is an urgent need for the development of hygienic amenities on the Highways to avoid accidents and improve road riding qualities. With the main focus on providing hygienic services and strengthening the National Highways, as well as improving the driving experience.


The provision of adequate roadside amenities is critical not only for improving road safety but also for reducing fatigue caused by travel. RIYO is intended to provide safe stopping points equipped with necessary amenities at regular intervals along the highways for truckers during their journey in order to reduce driving-related fatigue and make the journey more pleasant.



Developing Roadside Amenities With RIYO



RIYO is in the process of developing a policy on Wayside Amenities along the length of National Highways, which includes basic hygiene facilities. RIYO focuses on the Development of Road Side Amenities along National Highways and Major District Roads throughout the country to provide safe stopping points for highway entities such as truckers at regular intervals with required amenities such as eateries, restrooms, and truck stops.


During the journey, safe drinking water, clean toilets, parking space, rest room, and so on are provided to reduce driving-related fatigue and improve the driver's overall travel experience.



Key Objectives Of RIYO



RIYO encourages frequent breaks during the journey to reduce driver fatigue-related accidents by providing all necessary facilities at preferred Locations,


RIYO offers various job opportunities to deserving ones to boost the employment sector of our country.


RIYO offers a venue for local Truck drivers. By providing a safe location for truckers to park and spend their night in a hygienic environment.


RIYO provides First-aid and medical facilities as well.



Facilities at RIYO.


Food court with hygienic food both in veg or non-veg.


Wide space area for parking.


Truckers can recover from travel fatigue by staying in a room for a short period of time.


Dormitories for drivers/truckers with self-laundry facilities to provide adequate resting facilities to recover from travel fatigue.


Minor repair workshops, as well as an air filling station, puncture repair, pollution check, and spare parts shop.


Toe-ing Facility


Rest area with benches and tables in the open air


First aid boxes and medical facilities provide immediate medical assistance in the case of an accident



Summing Up


Truckers play a critical role in maintaining the economy of our country. They help in the transportation of life-saving medicine, equipment, and supplies. Also play a crucial role in the transport of essential goods to supermarkets, pharmacies, hospitals, and other critical services to various sectors within the country. Thus, it is important that the Highways must have better and clean amenities to offer hygienic services to truckers all over the country to maintain their healthy lifestyle on the Highways. RIYO is working closely to help the National Highways with this challenging task by providing the best hygienic services on the National Highways to ensure all the Truckers get hygienic services in a safe and healthy environment throughout their journey.
